Dataset Name:
Kaggle Superstore Sales Dataset

Columns used in this project:
1. Order Date – shows when the sale happened
2. Region – shows where the sale happened
3. Category – shows the product category
4. Sales – shows how much money was earned
5. Profit – shows profit or loss from sales

Why sales data visualization is important:
Sales data visualization helps us understand business performance,
identify trends, and compare sales across regions and products easily.
